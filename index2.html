Soto

A traditional Indonesian soup mainly composed of broth, meat, and vegetables.

Mendoan

A type of fried food that comes from the residency of Banyumas, Central Java, Indonesia.

Pecel

A traditional Javanese salad with peanut sauce, steamed rice, lontong or ketupat.

Rawon

An Indonesian beef soup originated from East Java. It has black keluak nut as the main seasoning, which gives a dark color and nutty flavor to the soup.

Rujak Cingur

A fruit salad which consists of various tropical fruits, tofu, and tempe, usually served with a spicy and sweet dressing.

Rendang

Originated in Minangkabau, it is a rich dish of meat that has been slow cooked and braised in a coconut milk seasoned with a herb and spice mixture.

Nasi Padang

A Minangkabau dish consists of steamed rice served with various choices of pre-cooked dishes, such as meats, vegetables, fish, and sambal.

Bakso

Indonesian meatball made from beef, served with yellow noodles and rice vermicelli.

Nasi Goreng

A Indonesian fried rice, served with fried egg and chicken.
